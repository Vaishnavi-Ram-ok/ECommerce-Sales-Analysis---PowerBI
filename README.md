 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard (PowerBI) provides an interactive E-Commerce Sales Dashboard built using Power BI, designed to analyze and visualize key business metrics such as sales, profit, customer behavior, and product performance.

Objective: 
To convert a raw ecommerce data into a visually appealling dashboard that provides insights about the sales, profits and product performance.

Key Features:
Sales Overview: Displays total revenue and profit with KPI cards for quick insights.
Geographical Analysis: Visualizes sales distribution across different states.
Time-Based Trends: Tracks profit trends over months, quarters, and years to identify seasonality and growth patterns.
Category Insights: Analyzes product categories and sub-categories contributing to profit.
Payment Mode Analysis: Breaks down customer preferences based on payment methods.
Interactive Visuals: Includes filters and dynamic charts for deeper data exploration.

Insights Gained:
Identified top-performing states and product categories.
Observed seasonal trends in sales and profit.
Analyzed customer purchasing behavior through quantity and payment modes.

Tools & Technologies:
Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
Data Modeling – Relationships and calculated measures
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Custom metrics and KPIs

Dataset:
The dataset includes order details such as:
Order ID
Product & Category
Sales Amount & Profit
Quantity
Payment Mode
Date & Location
